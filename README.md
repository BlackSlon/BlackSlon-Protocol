## Price Determination Formula
Our protocol utilizes a deterministic bonding curve to ensure transparent price settlement:

$$P = a \cdot e^{b \cdot S}$$

* **P (Current Price):** Real-time price of one Index Participation Token (IPT) (100 kWh).
* **a (Base Market Anchor):** The "starting point" pulled via decentralized oracles from physical exchanges/OTC.
* **e (Euler's Number):** Enables continuous growth modeling for a smooth price curve.
* **b (Sensitivity Factor):** The **Liquidty** Multiplier.
* **S (Supply):** Total IPTs held in the specific index pool.
